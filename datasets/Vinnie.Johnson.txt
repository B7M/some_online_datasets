Notes on fuller version of data
1.   No game = no game was played by the Pistons that day.
     No games scheduled = no games were scheduled for the entire NBA.

2.  The dates of the Pistons' last games vary from year to year
depending on whether they made it to the playoffs or not and to what
level in the playoffs.  That's why for example in the 1985-1986 season
the last game was played in April but in the 1988-1989 season the last
game was played in June.
  
3.  The dates of the scores correspond with the dates the scores that
were reported in the newspaper.  In other words the scores were
reported in the newspaper the day after the game was played.
